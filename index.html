SATYAM METRO QUEENS NECKLACE

Kharghar's most awaited launch is here Satyam developers have launched a new project in upper kharghar. Extraordinary living in ordinary price.Jodi flats are also available. Luxurious High Rise Towers With 4 Level Parking, Awesome Podium With All Amenities On 5th Floor. Environment clearance received for project and building permission received for project, RERA expected shortly. If you're planning to Buy your dream home in Kharghar, do visit this amazing project.
The iconic Queen's Necklace, also known as Marine Drive, has long been celebrated for its stunning crescent-shaped promenade along the Arabian Sea, adorned with twinkling city lights that resemble a necklace. This symbol of elegance, luxury, and timeless charm is all set to mesmerize homebuyers at Navi Mumbai as two of the city's finest developers recreate the Queen's Necklace in the form of a unique and contemporary residential project. Situated at Upper Kharghar, this project seeks to emulate the grace and allure of Queen's Necklace, replete with a 30,000 sq. ft. recreation zone and a terrace at a height of 265 ft. Come and be a part of this captivating living experience.
